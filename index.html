About

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Data Science at Sapienza University of Rome, where I am advised by Stefano Leonardi. Before that, I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at ETH Zurich in 2022, and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Princeton University in 2020.

I am broadly interested in Theoretical Computer Science, with a particular focus in Online Algorithms and Online Learning as well as Algorithmic Game Theory and Clustering.
