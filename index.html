Matteo Russo

I am a Ph.D. student in Data Science at Sapienza University of Rome, where I am advised by Stefano Leonardi.

My main research interests lie at the intersection between Computer Science and Economics, in particular Mechanism Design, Approximation and Online Algorithms, as well as Online Learning.
